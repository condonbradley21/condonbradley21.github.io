A little about my development history

After having a background in network engineering and getting certified from College of the Sequoias. I pursued a position at a data center in San Diego, CA. While working there I got the option to assist software developers and became interested in the development aspects of I.T. I studied web development and transferred over to an internship in downtown San Diego to refine my skills. From there I got my first contract position working with an Electronic Medical Records company. While there I learned how to work on mobile device apps and built the companies new website. After finishing my contract I got a new position working for a website marketing agency. While there I learned how to work production based wordpress layouts, producing 2-3 websites a week for dental offices and lawyers. For the past few years I have been freelancing, working with business owners building out custom e-commerce websites using wordpress and shopify platforms.
